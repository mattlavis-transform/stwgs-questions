Additional questions

  • Example 1 - 0602904700 (Conifers)
    There are 4 measures on this commodity that are all potentially suited to additional questions, as each of them has a single 'certificate' and a single 'waiver' associated with it.

  • Example 2 - 0208907000 (Frogs' legs)
    There are 4 measures on this commodity, only 2 of which are suited to additional questions: the other two (animal health certificate and veterinary control) are overly simple and complex respectively.

Questions to ask

Question Answer
How should the sequence of questions be defined? Do we care?  
Should the answers that are presented back be presented back in the same sequence as the questions were asked?  
What do we do about the measures where we do not ask a question, as there is only a single answer (e.g AHC on frogs' legs)  
What do we do about the measures where we do not ask a question, as it is too complex (e.g Vet control on frogs' legs)  
What do we do with the introductory text that is on the current licences and certificates page? Is it needed at all, in part or not at all, or does it need to be rewritten?  
What do we do with the document codes? These are crucial to this part of the journey to some users - there is no mockup.  
How do we differentiate the journey for the different user types (trader + dec, trader - dec, agent)  
Has consideration been given to the inclusion of much more complex text that is shown on the Figma prototype (examples in this prototype)  
How will Appendix 5a and 5b be embedded into this?  
We are presuming that the back control on the results page should go back to the last asked question. Correct?  
We are presuming that the back control on the question pages should go back to the manage-this-trade page or the previous question, depending on the part of the journey. Correct?  
We are presuming that clicking on the browser back button mimics the back control in all cases above. Correct?  
Action for the 'change answers' link - presume this shoul go back to the first question and there is no desire to remember answers & pre-populate questions?  
Bookmarking - what should happen if anyone chooses to bookmark a question page or a results page?  
Sharing - what happens when users want to share a page with a friend? What gets saved?  
Saving progress - How does this all fit in with the concept of saving progress / the task list component?
